Mutual Expectations Addendum (v2)

Trak Vault & Battalla

This Mutual Expectations Addendum ("Addendum") is entered into on April 15, 2026, and is supplemental to the Trak Vault Terms of Affiliation. This document outlines the specific promotional obligations and deliverables for the strategic partnership between Trak Vault and Battalla.

1. Trak Vault Promotional Obligations
  • In-App Advertising: Trak Vault agrees to host and run a digital advertising campaign for Battalla within the Trak Vault mobile application for a duration of one (1) year.
  • Dedicated Affiliate Section: Trak Vault will create and maintain a designated section for Battalla within the "Affiliates" area of the application.
  • B2B Promotion: For a duration of one (1) year, Trak Vault agrees to promote Battalla as a strategic affiliate to local card shops (LCS) during Trak Vault’s standard outreach and promotional efforts to such establishments.
  • Social Media Promotion: Trak Vault agrees to promote Battalla on its respective social media platforms at least one (1) time every two (2) months for the duration of the one (1) year term.
2. Battalla Promotional Obligations
  • Event & Convention Promotion: Battalla agrees to promote the Trak Vault application at all events and conventions they would otherwise attend. This includes, but is not limited to, distributing flyers, wearing Trak Vault branded merchandise, or hosting a small display.
  • Social Media Promotion: Battalla agrees to promote Trak Vault on its respective social media platforms at least one (1) time every two (2) months for the duration of the one (1) year term.
  • Future Program Participation: Battalla may be asked to participate in future Trak Vault initiatives, such as card or merchandise giveaways for Trak Vault users, as those programs become operational.
3. Term and Renewal

The specific obligations outlined in Section 1 and Section 2 shall remain in effect for one (1) year from the date of execution. Parties may review and renew these expectations upon mutual written agreement at the end of the term.

4. Integration

This Addendum is governed by the Trak Vault Terms of Affiliation. In the event of a conflict between this Addendum and the Terms of Affiliation, the terms of this Addendum shall prevail regarding the specific deliverables mentioned herein.
