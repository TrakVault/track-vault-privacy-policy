
MUTUAL EXPECTATIONS ADDENDUM: TRAK VAULT & BATTALLA (v2)
Effective Date: April 15, 2026

This Addendum is supplemental to the Trak Vault Terms of Affiliation and outlines the specific promotional exchange between Trak Vault and Battalla.

1. TRAK VAULT OBLIGATIONS
- Ad Campaign: Trak Vault will run a 1-year advertising campaign for Battalla within the Trak Vault app.
- App Integration: Trak Vault will create a dedicated section for Battalla in the "Affiliates" section of the app.
- B2B Referral: Trak Vault will promote Battalla as a strategic affiliate to local card shops during outreach for 1 year.
- Social Media: Trak Vault will promote Battalla on social media at least 1 time every 2 months for 1 year.

2. BATTALLA OBLIGATIONS
- In-Person Promotion: Battalla will promote Trak Vault at events and conventions they attend via flyers, merchandise, or displays.
- Social Media: Battalla will promote Trak Vault on social media at least 1 time every 2 months for 1 year.
- Future Participation: Battalla may be asked to participate in future programs (e.g., giveaways) as they become available.

3. DURATION
The promotional period is set for one (1) year.

4. WITHDRAWAL
Per Section 6 of the Terms of Affiliation, withdrawal is permitted provided all reciprocated efforts have been met.
